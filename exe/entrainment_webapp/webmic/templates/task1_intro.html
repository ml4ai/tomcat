ToMCAT Study

General instructions:

This section of the study has two speech recording tasks. For each recording task, when you are ready, you will need to:
  1. Add your ID number in the text box provided. Note that the recording will not begin without it. If you are unsure of your ID number, please contact a member of the research team to assist you.
  2. Press the ‘Start Recording’ button to begin each recording.
  3. Give permission to the browser to record audio, if prompted.
  4. Press the ‘Stop Recording’ button to end each task, once you are finished.
