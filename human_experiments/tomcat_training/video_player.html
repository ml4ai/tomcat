EEG / fNIRS / EKG / GSR
Setup Training Videos

  • 1. Head Measurements
    • All measurements are in centimeters (cm).
    • Measure head circumference.
    • Measure ear-to-ear.
    • Find head top center.
    • Record measurements.

  • 2. Selecting right size Cap
    • Review subject's head circumference as noted.
    • Review available cap sizes.
    • If subject's head circumference is between available cap sizes, decide whether to go with larger or smaller cap.

  • 3. Checking Cap fit on Subject
    • Measure ear-to-ear.
    • Noting subject's head top center.
    • Marking center forehead.
    • Fit cap on subject's head.

  • 4. Preparing Cap with EEG Electrodes - Part 1
    • Identify EEG GND holder on head-cap.
    • Snap EEG GND into head-cap.
    • Identify other electrodes positions.
    • Snap electrodes into head-cap.

  • 5. Preparing Cap with EEG Electrodes - Part 2
    • Continuing to snap electrodes into head-cap.
    • Route electrodes wires over head-cap.
    • Position EEG harness back of head-cap.

  • 6. Preparing Cap with fNIRS Sensors

    • Identify fNIRS ports on head-cap.
    • Insert sensor into head-cap port.
    • Identify which sensor housing spring strength to use.
      (There are different housings with different spring strength in them. The spring strength will be indicated by a Roman Numeral on top of housing,
      0 = lowest strength, I = medium, II = highest strength).
    • Snap sensor holder onto sensors.
    • Continue to place all sensors into there specified head-cap ports and snap sensor specified housing onto sensors.
    • Route sensor wires over head-cap.
    • Position fNIRS harness back of head-cap.

  • 7. Powering Up EEG Computer
    • Verify both EEG computer monitors are powered on.
    • Locate the computer's power button.
    • Power on the computer.
    • Log in the computer with user "LabWorker".
    • Note the "EEG Start Up Procedure" popup.

  • 8. Connecting Batteries to EEG Amps
    • Locate batteries on charging stations.
    • Make sure battery is fully charged with charging errors.
      (Charger should have solid green light).
    • Remove one battery at a time from chargers.
      (3 batteries will be needed, Lion, Tiger, and Leopard).
    • Battery will sit underneath Amp.
    • Connect battery to EEG Amp.
      (Batteries must be connected in sequence,
      Lion first, Tiger second, Leopard last).
    • Within seconds of connecting battery, the Red LED on top of Amp should flash once indicating,
      that the Amp has power and is initialized.
